Non Proportional Symbol Map Visualization

Encoding

For this visualization, Request Type is encoded with color and it represents different type of request made in Category Park and Rec. Longitude and Latitude are encoded in basemap. Details are shown in tooltip. Each circle represent one row of complaint for park and rec from SF 311Cases dataset.

Wrangling

Original dataset is updating everyday. It consist of 20 columns and more than 400K rows in it. It has various categories. The dataset which is used in visualization is filtered based on Opened Date as month of February, 2020 and Category is Park / Rec.

Interactivity

Implemented Details on Demand and brushing technique as an interactivity. Here is the .gif of it to show demonstration of it.

Conclusion

From this visualization, it is conclude and observed that most of the cases are Structural Maintenance near Golden Gate Park encoded with greenish color and all over SF there are maximum cases of Park-Patrol encoded in orange color. It has least cases for Urban Forestry.
